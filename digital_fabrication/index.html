Assignment 1

Modelling and Laser Cutting!

This project explores how to operate a laser cutter and how to generate models for the machine to use. It is a kit of 30 cardboard instances of one part that can be press-fit together and survive a gentle shaking.

Assignment 2

Parametric Modelling with Grasshopper!

Motivated by a dinosaur mask, this project is intended to unfold over time as a dragon puppet. I wanted to make scales like a dragon has and ultimately accomplished this but not on a dragon...on a cylinder. So: here I am modelling a polygon to interlink with copies of itself around a cylinder.
